// About Me

柯俊宇 Hugo Ke

20 年以上設計資歷，專注於金融科技、企業級 SaaS、房仲 PropTech 領域的數位產品設計。從用戶研究、資訊架構到高保真原型，具備完整的端到端設計流程經驗。

曾參與中國信託、兆豐銀行、安聯人壽、亞太電信等知名企業的大型 APP 專案，從訪談、與 PM 共同規劃到 UI 設計驗收皆有完整的流程參與。擅長跨部門溝通，能與工程師、PM、業務等不同角色高效協作。

積極擁抱 AI 工具融入設計流程，運用 ChatGPT、Claude、Midjourney 等工具加速概念發想與設計產出。樂於分享設計新知，推動團隊設計文化。

// Design Philosophy 設計哲學

「選擇漸進式優化，而非全面重設計。」面對多元使用者背景時，我相信以用戶為中心的迭代式改善，比追求一步到位的完美更能創造持續的價值。好的設計不在於顛覆，而在於每一次迭代都讓體驗更好一點。

20+ years of design experience specializing in FinTech, enterprise SaaS, and PropTech digital product design. End-to-end capability from user research and information architecture to high-fidelity prototyping.

Collaborated with leading enterprises including CTBC Bank, Mega Bank, Allianz Life, and Asia Pacific Telecom on large-scale app projects — from user interviews and PM collaboration to UI design delivery. Skilled at cross-functional communication with engineers, PMs, and business stakeholders.

Actively integrating AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Midjourney into design workflows to accelerate ideation and production. Passionate about sharing design knowledge and fostering team design culture.

// Design Philosophy

"Incremental refinement over full redesign." When facing diverse user backgrounds, I believe user-centered iterative improvement creates more sustained value than pursuing one-shot perfection. Great design isn't about disruption — it's about making the experience a little better with every iteration.
